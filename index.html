About the Artist

A storyteller behind the lens.

Hello — I'm Sophia, a fine-art bridal photographer with over a decade of experience capturing the quiet, breathtaking moments that define a wedding day.

My approach blends gentle direction with documentary intuition: the way the morning light traces your veil, the laugh shared between you and your maid of honor, the held breath before the first look. Every frame is composed to feel like a memory you can step back into.

Based in the Pacific Northwest and available for destination weddings worldwide.

— Sophia
